ALGORITHM ---

1. Import all required libraries.
2. Load the Twitter dataset and remove missing tweets.
3. Clean each tweet (lowercase, remove URLs, stopwords, etc.).
4. Keep three sentiment classes and convert them to numeric labels.
5. Split dataset into training and testing sets.
6. Convert tweets to TF-IDF numeric features.
7. Train a KNN classifier using cosine distance.
8. Predict sentiments on test data and evaluate accuracy.
9. Plot confusion matrix.
